Import birth records — Military Hospital Riyadh
Paste the birth extract (one infant per line: Name, MRN, DOB (YYYY-MM-DD), Weight kg, Mother phone) or load the demo batch. The AI engine de-duplicates against the registry, computes eligibility and the weight-based nirsevimab dose, and queues mother outreach.
🤖
What the import engine does
Local · no external API
De-duplicates by MRN against the registry
Computes eligibility: <8 months = first-season dose
Weight-based dose: <5 kg → 50 mg · ≥5 kg → 100 mg
Queues a mother-outreach Care Task per eligible infant
🇸🇦 Saudi RSV policy (nirsevimab)
First season: all infants <8 months — one dose (<5 kg → 50 mg, ≥5 kg → 100 mg).

Second season: high-risk children 8–19 months — one 200 mg dose.

Season: ~October to March. SFDA-approved Feb 2024.
